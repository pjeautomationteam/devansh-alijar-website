The person

Since 2019

He made his first
jewellery sale at nine.

Some people find their purpose. Devansh was born into his.

Third-generation jeweller from one of Hyderabad's oldest jewellery families. He grew up in the business — watching how trust is built across generations, how a single misinformed purchase can break it in minutes. He chose transparency over transaction.

A BSc in Business Management from the University of London brought structure to intuition. Returning to India, he became CMO at Panna Jewellers Exclusive — where he's rebuilding a 180-year legacy for the next 180 years.

But Devansh doesn't sit still. He's an author. An investor. Someone who completed Ironman 70.3 at 18, cycled from Manali to Leh, Kashmir to Kanyakumari, trekked to Annapurna and Everest Base Camp. The same discipline that gets you up a mountain gets your brand to the top.
